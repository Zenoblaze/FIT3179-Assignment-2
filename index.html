Esports Across the Globe

Esports are a growing phenonmenon with prize pools similar to that of certain modern day sports such as Tennis, basketball or golf But what are esports?

Esports Globally

Esports are not just contained to western countries, with players from all over the world being able to particpate in tournaments online or in person. Commonly Esport tournaments are help in person to reduce lag, which refers to a delay in response from the game after an action has been issued. Although these delays are normally only several milliseconds, this difference is massive for people at the highest level of competitve play.

Esports Teams & Players

Although funding from tournaments may be the most obvious way that professional esport players can earn money, just like more traditional sports there are esport organisation, teams, and players.

Some of the biggest esport teams generally have sponsors with different technology companis as a large source of revenue. In return for representing the sponsor companies by wearing clothes with their logos, or using their gear, esport teams are reimbursed. In this manner the team takes a large portion and pays out their players depending on their contracts.
